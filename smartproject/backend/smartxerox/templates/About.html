🖨️ About Us – Smart Xerox Service


Welcome to Smart Xerox Service, your go-to platform for fast, efficient, and hassle-free document printing. We are a technology-driven solution built to modernize the traditional photocopy and document printing experience. In today's fast-paced world, waiting in queues for Xerox or printouts is time-consuming and frustrating. Smart Xerox Service solves this by offering a fully online platform where users can upload documents, select print preferences, make secure payments, and either schedule a pickup or get the documents delivered—without ever stepping into a shop.


💡 What We Offer:


📁 Online Document Upload – Upload PDFs, Word files, or images from your device or cloud storage.
🖨️ Custom Print Options – Choose page range, single/double-sided, color or B/W, paper size, and more.
💳 Secure Online Payments – Pay via UPI, cards, or wallets with invoice generation.
🧾 Instant Order Tracking – Know your order status in real-time.
🚚 Pickup or Delivery Options – Choose doorstep delivery or local store pickup.
🔒 Document Security – Your files are encrypted and automatically deleted after processing.
